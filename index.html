Portfolio

The Hobbit Model and Texture

this project uses real world images of a book i have, textured into Maya as a 3D object and rendered along with a small ring that pertains to the story. by cropping images i took on my phone onto a texture map i was able to accurately model this book into its real life dimensions.

Insignia

this insignia was the product of my desire to make something simple yet reflective of the capitalist system, with those in the middle of the hierarchy seeking to move always upward and those at the top consuming the products of those below while always looking down on the lesser tiers.

Frog Hunt

this project shows a lets play of a VR game made in a two person team where the user is transported into a swamp with the task of collecting frogs for an old man. the strange visions and an ambiguous intro, it's hard to tell if the swamp environment is real or part of the player's imagination.

Ceramic Coaster

this coaster is a print of a collage made in photoshop and baked onto a ceramic tile, with a few rubber bumpers beneath it makes for a pleasing and artful coaster depicting a house hidden beneath mushrooms deep in the forest.

Gumball Machine Animation

This project is a scene of a character attempting to get a gumball out of a gumball machine, failing at first and using violent means to try to dislodge the gumball, only after treating the machine kindly does the gumball fall out. the focus of this animation was to show the characters emotions through their actions as well as having camera work that displays their whole process.

CD Mockup

this CD mockup was made for a local band with little direction except the album name. i used a image i took while in Mexico of the beach near Costa Maya. Using a hue shift and saturation tuning i was able to give the image a bright, vaporwave-type feel

Wizard Box Design

this Design is meant to be laser cut into a wooden box, with different characters on each side and an open top. the characters are wizards i drew and their various hat decorations are meant to be boldly engraved into the wood

Sleeper Shark Infographic

this personal project was made to be a deep dive into the lifestyle and habits of the Sleeper Shark. assets were created in Illustrator and the project was put together in After Effects. the script and research was probably the easiest part of the project while drawing the environment took rather more time.

"Lucky" Animation

this project was a collaboration to make a short film depicting a black cat that is unwanted no matter where he goes, eventually finding someone like him (puppet animated) that will care for him. my roll was compiling and editing the media together seamlessly as well was keying out the green screen used for the puppets.

Panda attack

panda attack is a simple javascript project i made in which small pandas of various colors spawn in a field and slowly grow larger, giving the appearance of coming toward the user in a jittery motion. the user can click on the pandas on the screen to make them disappear but more will spawn indefinitely.

Sword in stone animation

This projects displays a short animation of a character pulling a sword from a stone with some difficulty. the focus was on exaggerated actions and lifelike movements. the animation features a comical twist as when the sword loosens, it is shown to be much longer than one would think and causes the character to fall to the ground.

Smoking Supercut

this is a clip combining project i made to highlight the difference between older cigarette ads that aired in the US and more modern anti-smoking ads meant to create a humorous yet insightful view of society's view of smoking has changed over the years

LEGO Saturn V Build

This project was a step by step building animation of the LEGO saturn five rocket ship done by me and a friend. taking up over a week of shooting and building the greatest difficulty we faced was keeping everything in its relative place between shoots as well as Keying out the background in post production.

Groove Design

This design was made using type characters to create bold outlines and with a colorful gradient it gives a sense of musical intent. the gradient colors above and below inversely mirror each other on each level giving some symmetry to the piece. it somewhat resembles a record on a turntable but could be interpreted in many ways

Lighting Work

UNCA Spring Fling 2022

I was commissioned to provide lighting for bands playing during the UNCA Spring Fling in 2022. the stage was the grotto inside the Highsmith Student Union building on UNCA campus and without any way of hanging fixtures i had to use creative ways to light the artists without blinding them.

Solasta 2022

I was part of a team that built and ran the lighting for the center stage of the Solasta festival in 2022. using a combination of traditional fixtures and LEDs as well as natural elements like sugarcane branches we highlighted the DJs playing their sets for one of the biggest EDM festivals in WNC.

New Years Party in Marshall NC

I was commissioned to set up, design, and run lighting for one of several dance rooms for an exclusive party hosted on an island in Marshall NC for New Years 2024. using a fixture setup and a fog machine i created a club like environment that fitted the music and vibe of the party

Marty Stuart and his Fabulous Superlatives

This event was held near Carrier Park in Asheville NC featuring famous Bluegrass and Country music star Marty Stuart . the venue was an open field with a mobile stage which provided a lot of tricky design and setup issues.
